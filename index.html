About Us

Welcome to Sasta Csi, a technical club dedicated to demystifying the world of technology, from the fundamentals of coding to advanced concepts in cybersecurity, AI, and software development. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to learn, collaborate, and innovate, turning complex "bugs" into elegant and brilliant solutions.

We offer workshops, coding challenges, guest lectures, and hands-on projects designed to equip members with practical skills and foster a strong technical community. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting your journey, Sasta Csi is the place to explore your passion for technology and build your future.
